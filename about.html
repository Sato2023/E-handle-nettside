About

After years of experimenting, we have finally managed to find a lip gloss formula that we love - and hopefully you will love it too! Our passion for beauty is one of the reasons why Rhosegold was found. We are here to make YOU feel confident and beautiful.

All of our lip glosses are vegan, cruelty-free, gluten free and handmade with love. Our lip glosses are non-sticky, moisturising and infused with vitamin E to hydrate your lips. We will always focus on improving our formula as we grow our product range.

RhoseGold is for everyone.
