FAQ

When will my order arrive?

The processing time is 1-5 business days and shipping will usually take 3-5 business days. Most of the time, an order will be prosessen within 24 hours after the order was placed.

What are the ingredients in your products?

The ingredients may differ from every lip product, please check the description of each product to see all the ingredients.

Are your products vegan and cruelty free?

Some products willl restock (excluding limited edition products etc.). Restock dates vary depending on the product availability.

What are the payment methods?

VIPPS, VISA, MasterCard & American Express

How can I pay through VIPPS?

When placing an order, choose «Offline Payment» to pay with VIPPS and follow the instructions.

How much is shipping?

29 NOK within Norway and 55 NOK to Denmark and Sweden

What is Pick-Up?

We offer pick-up as a delivery method where you can pick up your order at a location - no shipping costs. You can request a pick-up time when placing the order. You will receive an e-mail when the order is ready and a confirmation of pick-up date & time. If you do not request a pick-up time, don’t worry, we will reach out!

Why haven’t I received a confirmation e-mail?

Please check your junk mails for order confirmation e-mail, shipping confirmation e-mail etc.
